Explore the environmental impacts of Colorado Wildfires


After the 2020 wildfires, CSU researchers, community members and government collaborators mobilized to better understand the short- and long-term impacts of severe, high-elevation fires. This portal acts as an open-source forum for connecting that data, facilitating collaboration and sharing the fire impact findings to a broader community.
